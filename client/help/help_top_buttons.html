Top Buttons

4. Top Buttons

1).Share to Facebook: After linked to Facebook, users may share this chat room to his/her Facebook friends. More details

2).Share to Twitter: After linked to Twitter, users may share this chat room to his/her Twitter followers . More details

3).Publish my video: Use it to publish your video (provided that there is a webcam plugged in and the session is not taken yet by some other application). More details

or, Start Broadcasting: When the Video Conference Module is enabled instead of the regular video chat mode, video publishers become broadcasters, whose video will automatically broadcast for everyone in the current chat room. More details

or, Publish PPM video: PPM means pay per minute or per time. In a PPM enabled chat room, the broadcasters predefined by the chat owner are entitled to publish PPM video and charge for it. More details

4).Send gift: Use it to send gifts to other users. More details

5).Settings: Function buttons are all in the drop-down list of the Settings button. See the picture above.

Whiteboard: Whether to express your idea with creative drawing, or just draw randomly with others, whiteboard may help you.

Turn off sound: You may turn off all the sounds of the chat room when you need it.

Scrolling: Start or stop scrolling the chat screen automatically. If enabled, the latest message will appear right above the input area; if disabled, the chat messages will be pinned there so that you can examine the words carefully.

Open chat history: The past conversation history can be found here, from the moment that you've entered the chat room. Click Public Log to read the public messages in the chat lobby of a certain amount, defined by your admin.

Clear Screen: It will erase all the messages on the screen.

Exit Room: To exit the current room, but neither log out nor disconnect.

Facebook: After linked to Facebook, use it to invite Facebook friends to this chat room.

Twitter: After linked toTwittetr, use it to invite Twitter followers to this chat room.

Tweet Chat: After linked to Twitter, use it to synchronize your public chat messages with the Twitter account. Details

Help: Use it to open Help introduction documents.

6).About: The chat software information.
