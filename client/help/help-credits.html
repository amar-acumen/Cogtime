Virtual Currency

Virtual Currency

1. Credits Service
Credits are the virtual currency in all of the 123 Flash Chat rooms, although the different chat owners may decide different cash credit ratio on their own. It’s the fundamental for PPM and virtual gifts.

How to buy credits?
Registered members of any 123 Flash Chat rooms can purchase credits, either by clicking the shopping cart icon above the user list, or at the time they try to enter a Pay Per Minute room or watch a Pay Per Minute video, or send a gift, but don't have enough credits.

Guests will be asked to register first to be able to buy chat credits. Or if admin set the earn credits, even a guest may click ads(offered by BCC partners) to get credits, fast and easy, and it's free!

Where to buy credits?
Buychatcredits.com (BCC) will be dealing with all the chat credits purchases across the world, BCC is the credits service provider as well as a subsidiary of 123 Flash Chat.

The working scenario: Once a chat owner of 123 Flash Chat software enables the credits service, he/she will get a BCC account and password combination, and becomes BCC affiliate automatically and start earning immediately!

Then his/her members may buy credits on BCC and the chat owner gets most of the revenue.

What can I do with credits?
Members can redeem credits for virtual gifts and send to each other, which makes a strong community. Virtual Gifts details.
They can also use credits to enter a Pay Per Minute chat room or to watch a Pay Per Minute video. PPM Details.
However, credits can be redeemed for cash.

All credits activities can be viewed in My Account -> Credits Activities.

Gifts In: The list of your received gifts history.
Gifts Out: The list of your sent gifts history.
PPM Video: The list of your watched PPM Video history.
PPM Room: The list of your entered PPM Room history.
Recharge: The list of your recharge history.
