PPM Video/Room

PPM Video/Room

Members are required to pay credits per minute or per time to watch a member's video or enter a chat room, that's what PPM is about, and it will be an efficient tool for chat owners to make tangible profits from the video chat business .
If a member is watching a Pay Per Minute video, a timer and the corresponding amount of credits will be displayed to the member while video chat is in progress. And the timer will be updated every second.
*PPM Room:
Users can’t enter a PPM room, only members who have enough credits can enter. So register first and buy credit to enter a PPM room.

The PPM room has two charge types: Pay per Minute and Pay per Time.
Pay per Minute: The users will pay per minute to enter the chat room.
Pay per Time: The users will pay per time to enter the chat room.

How to view my PPM Room History?
Open My Account panel to check the credits activities, PPM Room.

Room id: The PPM room you entered.
Start Time: The start time you entered the PPM room.
End Time: The end time you existed the PPM room.
Type: PPM or PPT, that is Pay per Minute or Pay per Time.
Receiver: The commission earner of the PPM room.
Sender: The username of those who entered the PPM room.
Credits: For chat owner, he/she will see “+” in credits, which is the increased credits; For other members, they will see “-” in credits, which is the cost of PPM room they paid.

*PPM Video:
Members have to pay credits to watch the PPM video.

How to publish PPM video?
Enable PPM video in admin panel -> room settings, and set the broadcaster who can publish PPM video in the chat room.

After publishing successfully, the PPM video icon will show in userlist. When someone click the PPM video icon, the commission credits will add to broadcaster.

The PPM video has two charge types: Pay per Minute and Pay per Time.
Pay per Minute: The users will be charged per minute to watch PPM video.
After your agreement, the PPM video will pop up, and the chat window will show the charged credits amount.

Pay per Time: The users will be charged for per time to watch PPM video.
Click OK to confirm pay credits and open the PPM video window.

Who can publish PPM video?
Just those broadcasters who are predefined by the chat owner can publish PPM video and charge for it, others can publish regular video only.

How to check my PPM Video viewing history?
Open My Account panel to check the credits activities, PPM Video.

Room id: The room where you watched PPM video.
Start Time: The start time you watched the PPM video.
End Time: The end time you closed the PPM video.
Type: PPM or PPT, that is Pay per Minute or Pay per Time.
Receiver: The broadcaster of the PPM video.
Sender: The username of those who watched the PPM video.
Credits: For broadcaster, he/she will see “+” in credits, which is the commission credits they get, and “-” means the credits they paid to watch PPM video; For other members, they will see “-” in credits, which is the cost of PPM video they paid.
